1. Types of Data Collected

At Chart AI, we collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

1.1. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and other information provided during account creation.
  • Profile Information: Username, profile picture, and demographic information.
  • Preferences: Market type, risk level, analysis depth, trading style, timeframe, and indicator preferences within the application.
  • User Content: Charts, drawings, and other images uploaded for analysis.
  • Communication Data: Information provided when contacting our support team.
  • Subscription Information: Payment details and transaction history if you subscribe to premium features (processed by our payment processors, not stored directly by us).

1.2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, device identifiers, and browser information.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the application, analysis history, login dates and duration.
  • Location Information: General geographic location (at the country or city level only).

  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Cookies and similar technologies used to enhance your in-app experience.
Note: Chart AI may collect partially anonymized analytical data. This data is used to improve our services and is anonymized to the extent possible.

5. Data Security

Protecting your information is a priority at Chart AI. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5.1. Security Measures

  • Encryption: We encrypt data in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols.
  • Access Controls: We restrict access to personal information to authorized employees, contractors, and service providers who need to know that information to operate, develop, or improve our application.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Secure Authentication: We use secure authentication methods to protect user accounts.
  • Monitoring: We continuously monitor our systems for potential vulnerabilities and attacks.

5.2. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

  • Chart data and analysis history are retained for 12 months after creation, unless you delete them earlier.

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by applicable law. Our notification will include information about the breach, affected data, and steps we are taking to mitigate potential harm.

Note on Security Limitations: While we take reasonable measures to protect your personal information, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data transmitted to our application; any transmission is at your own risk.
